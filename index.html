Quick rules

  • Roll dice on your turn. Your token walks to the destination cell-by-cell.
  • If you land on an unowned property, you can buy it for the listed price.
  • If you land on an owned property, you pay rent. Rent doubles on full color groups, then increases with houses (1â†’4) and a hotel.
  • Once you own a full color group, you can build houses. You must build evenly across the group.
  • Three doubles in a row, or landing on "Go to Jail", sends you to jail. Pay $50 or roll doubles to leave.
  • Pass GO to collect $200.
  • Last player not bankrupt wins.

Tips

  • Hover any property on the board to see its title deed with current rent and owner.
  • Owning a full color group doubles rent immediately, even with zero houses.
  • Keep a cash buffer â€” landing on someone else's hotel can wipe you out.
  • Mortgaging gives you half the price back. Unmortgaging costs 10% interest.
